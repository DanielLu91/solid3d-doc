◆ DeleteEntities()

static ResultStatusEnum S3D::Entity::DeleteEntities ( const std::vector< Entity * > & entities)
static

If a geometric entity is used in the definition of another geometric entity (e.g. base curve of an trimmed curve), then it may not be deleted unless that other entity is included in the array.
.

When deleting geometric entities, their geometric dependents will also be deleted if they no longer have any geometric or topological owners.
